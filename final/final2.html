The castle that Beaty and the Beast is replicated after this castle. We got a tour and took lots of beautiful photos with the castle behind us. The next couple days we spent in Munich. Munich was my favorite place on our trip. We went to the BMW manufracture, went to the old kings summer home, and got a tour of the beautuful architecture that Hilter wanted in his capital city. We had lots of free time. We walked into a beer garden where there were thousands of people enjoying a drink with their friends and family.

Both places were beautuful and I always wanted to go back when I got older. This trip was perfect. We worked with an organization called EF Educational Tours. Each day we wwent of safaris and got to see the beautiful animals in the famous national parks. We went there with my moms friend from college and her two kids who were around my age.

I have been so fortunite to be able to travel as much as I have. I have seen the world through so many lenses and it has made me more of a globel citizen. I urge everyone to travel outside of the US and see how different countries do different things. It is so cool to see how other people live and see how different other cultures are.
